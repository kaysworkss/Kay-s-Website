Apoti Olowe — Digital Sculpture

New Series · 2026

A love story of Ayo, a wood carver, and Morenike, his lover — told through paint, wood, and care.

Six main paintings advance the storyline. Each painting arrives with a set of digital sculptures present within its setting — each digital sculpture carrying its own story, meaning, hidden clues, and foreshadowing of the chapter to come.

The auctions for each painting and its companion digital sculptures will be announced ahead of every drop, presented in series with weeks between each release.

The Artist

A little
about me

"I make things because the world makes more sense through a mark on paper than it does in words."

a multidisciplinary artist from Nigeria. Through photography, portraiture, sketching, painting, and digital art, I explore human emotion, connection, and the quiet moments people often overlook.

I've been creating for as long as I can remember, and I'm driven by the belief that art should feel like it comes from somewhere real. My work is inspired by everyday interactions — the subtle gestures, unspoken feelings, and small details that reveal who we are.
