Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀ — Digital Sculpture

New Series · 2026

A love story of Ayo, a wood carver, and Morenike, his lover — told through paint, wood, and care.

Six main paintings advance the storyline. Each painting arrives with a set of digital sculptures present within its setting — each digital sculpture carrying its own story, meaning, hidden clues, and foreshadowing of the chapter to come.

    The Artist

    A little
    about me

    "I make things because the world makes more sense when I express my thoughts with my art."

    I am a multidisciplinary artist from Nigeria. Through photography, portraiture, sketching, painting, and digital art, I explore human emotion, connection, and the quiet moments people often overlook.

    I've been creating for as long as I can remember, and I'm driven by the belief that art should feel like it comes from somewhere real. My work is inspired by people and simplicity, I try to capture stories and experiences in simple yet cool ways. There is so much beauty around us and I want to bring them to your notice.
