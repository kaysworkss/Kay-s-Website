Frequently asked

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to follow it. Nigerian orders ship via GIG Logistics — track at giglogistics.com with your waybill number. International orders ship via DHL or UPS — track on the carrier's site (dhl.com or ups.com) with the air waybill number in your email. If you haven't received tracking within 3 business days of ordering, message us and we'll chase it up.
We accept cards (debit/credit) through Paystack — which handles both Naira and US dollar payments — and cryptocurrency: ETH and stablecoins (USDC, USDT) on Ethereum, plus XTZ on Tezos. Card payments are processed securely on Paystack's page; crypto payments are confirmed on-chain before your order is finalised. Choose your method at checkout.

International orders ship from Nigeria via DHL and UPS — chosen for their global reach and customs handling, typically delivering to most countries within a few business days. Prints ship flat in a rigid mailer; large prints ship rolled in a protective tube. Duties and import taxes are set by your country and are the buyer's responsibility — the carrier will contact you if anything is due on arrival. A per-region delivery rate is shown at checkout before you pay.

Shop Config

Products

Featured this month

Choose which print is spotlighted in the homepage-style featured section. Visitors will be taken to its detail page if it has a slug.

Discount codes

Create codes customers can apply at checkout. Scope decides what the percentage comes off: Products (item subtotal only), Shipping (delivery only), or Everything (both). A 100%-off code with scope Everything is a free test order — it skips payment entirely and records as paid.

Tip: add a code like DEV100 at 100% / Everything to test the full checkout flow (including shipping) without spending anything. Disable it before going live.

Crypto wallet addresses

These are loaded from the shop_config row on the server — the same record the server verifies incoming payments against, so the displayed address can never differ from the one being checked. To change them, update eth_address / tezos_address in that row.

Payment keys (set as env vars)

Set PAYSTACK_PUBLIC_KEY in your Vercel environment variables. It is injected at runtime by the page — never stored here.
