Entry Token Hub

Mint your way in. Grow with the series.

Start by minting the Entry Token on the chain you prefer. Each Entry Token gives you access to multi-chain auctions and exclusive claims, and holding five wooden Entry Tokens lets you redeem a Bronze Token with special collector privileges.

After minting on Ethereum or Tezos, return here to verify ownership and request the equivalent Entry Token on the other chain. You do not need to double-mint to participate across both ecosystems.
