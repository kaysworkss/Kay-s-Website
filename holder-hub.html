The Opa Collection

Three sculptures, each cast digitally and viewable in the round — rotate, zoom, and look closely at the surface work. Opa means "staff" — each piece carries a different chapter of the Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀ story forward.

Opa I — "Opa Orisun"

Opa Orisun

Available $220 · Edition of 1

Digitally sculpted, cast in bronze-toned resin. 34cm at full height.

How it ties into the story One of the very first staffs Ayo ever carved — Orisun means "source" or "origin." It marks the beginning of his craft, before the mastery seen in later chapters.

Opa Iranti
