Auction Access Going Forward

A note on phased auction access and the collector privileges that continue with your token.

Project note July 2026

A note to holders

Dear Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀ holders,

First, thank you. Your belief in this project has meant a great deal. By collecting and holding these access tokens, you helped support the foundation of Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀ at a very important stage, and I do not take that lightly.

What's changing

Until now, holding a token has meant bid-and-buy access to the chapter auctions, alongside benefits like your name on the project monolith — with Bronze also granting merch access. Going forward, auctions will follow a phased format, and that access becomes a real head start rather than just an entry ticket.

Token holders will always be given the first opportunity to bid before an auction opens to the wider public. This holder-first window will run for a full week, giving you real time to act rather than a token gesture. It is designed to protect the value of your early support while still giving each work a clear private window before it continues into public access.

  • For selected main painting auctions, the private holder phase may open with a lower reserve than the public phase. This means holders can receive a meaningful early advantage if they choose to bid during their access window.
  • For sculpture sets and certain special works, the reserve will remain the same in both phases, but holders still get first access before the public.
  • I'll indicate ahead of each auction which category it falls into, so you know what to expect going in.
If, for any reason, the private window closes without a bid, the work will simply continue its journey into public access.

This change is not about reducing the role of the tokens. It is the opposite. The tokens continue to carry their privileges, and this phased structure is designed to make that early access count for more.

Collector benefits

Your tokens also carry tiered merch benefits:

  • Bronze token holders receive one free tote per Bronze token held, plus up to 25% off available merch such as totes, hoodies, and T-shirts.
  • Wood token holders receive up to 15% off available merch.

And beyond the auctions and merch, your tokens remain part of the long-term structure of the project: the project monolith, the key pieces, and the end-stage vision of Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀.

The intention is simple: your tokens should give you priority, access, and recognition, while each work is given the chance to find its owner.

Thank you again for being part of this from the inside. I am grateful for the trust, the patience, and the belief you have shown. As Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀ continues to unfold, holders will remain part of its core story.

With respect,
Kay
