Built for Collectors

Milestones & Future Plans

What the Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀ Project has already shipped—and where the collector experience goes next.

Milestone 01

Live Collector Chatrooms

Collectors can speak with one another in real time while bidding, turning each auction into a shared live experience.

A first in the NFT space
Milestone 02

Holder-Gated Auctions

Dedicated auctions can be reserved for verified token holders of the project.

Milestone 03

Cross-Chain Auctions

Ethereum and Tezos collectors can participate in the same artwork auctions without being divided by chain.

A first of its kind
Milestone 04

Price Lock

Reserve bid values remain consistent even when the market price of the underlying coin fluctuates.

Milestone 05

Private Offers

Collectors can propose ownership terms outside the standard auction mechanism.

Milestone 06

Auction Hall of Fame

Past bidding histories and auction interactions are preserved for every piece as part of the project archive.

Milestones & Future Plans

What the Àpótí Ọlọ́wẹ̀ Project has already shipped—and where the collector experience goes next.

Milestone 01

Live Collector Chatrooms

Collectors can speak with one another in real time while bidding, turning each auction into a shared live experience.

A first in the NFT space
Milestone 02

Holder-Gated Auctions

Dedicated auctions can be reserved for verified token holders of the project.

Milestone 03

Cross-Chain Auctions

Ethereum and Tezos collectors can participate in the same artwork auctions without being divided by chain.

A first of its kind
Milestone 04

Price Lock

Reserve bid values remain consistent even when the market price of the underlying coin fluctuates.

Milestone 05

Private Offers

Collectors can propose ownership terms outside the standard auction mechanism.

Milestone 06

Auction Hall of Fame

Past bidding histories and auction interactions are preserved for every piece as part of the project archive.
